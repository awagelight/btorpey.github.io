Beyond Compare

Jan 29th, 2014

I’ve been a happy user of Beyond Compare on Windows for many years, so one of my big concerns when switching to Mac a while ago was, what was I going to use in its place?

Well, the gods smiled on me — Scooter Software released an alpha version of Beyond Compare for Mac right around the time I made the switch. (And a good thing, too! I tried everything else out there, and nothing else even comes close).

So, what is Beyond Compare? It’s a comparison program that does visual “diffs” of directories and folders. I use it to backup and sync my machines, for reviewing changes prior to commit, and in general just to keep track of what I’ve changed as I work. If you’ve never used a visual diff like BC, trust me, you don’t know what you’re missing.

It’s now out in public beta, and comes highly recommended (http://www.scootersoftware.com/beta.php) — it’s saved my tuchis more times than I can count.
